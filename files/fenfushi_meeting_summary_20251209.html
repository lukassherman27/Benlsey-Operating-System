üè† Villa MEP Rooms - New Approach

Previous Design: Shared MEP room between two villas
New Design: Individual MEP room attached to each villa behind outdoor shower area
Rationale: Landscape boundary walls removed from villas, outdoor shower now enclosed with solid walls. MEP room can utilize existing solid wall structure - just add wall extension and roof. Significantly cheaper than standalone MEP buildings.
Equipment to house: Swimming pool pump + electrical panels (AC outdoor units handled separately)
For 2BR Villas (V2A, V3A): Can have pop-outs on both sides of outdoor shower area. ~1m wall space available on each side.
